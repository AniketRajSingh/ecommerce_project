About Snehsattva Oils

Bringing back the goodness of traditional, natural, and chemical-free edible oils.

At Snehsattva Oils, we are on a mission to make healthy living affordable for everyone. Our journey began with a focus on providing food choices that our ancestors consumed for thousands of years. We believe in preserving the true nutrition, flavor, and aroma of edible oils.

Our oils are made from the finest quality food-grade seeds sourced from farms across India. With a 24x7 processing capacity in a pollution-free environment, we ensure that our products reach you fresh and pure.
